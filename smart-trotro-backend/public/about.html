About Smart-Trotro

We're building the future of urban mobility in Ghana — one trotro at a time. Smarter, safer, and more transparent public transport for everyone.

Our Story

From Chaos to Clarity

Smart-Trotro was born out of a simple frustration — the unpredictability of Ghana's trotro system. Millions of Ghanaians rely on trotros daily, yet there's no way to know when one will arrive, what the fare should be, or how to send a parcel reliably.

We set out to change that. By combining GPS tracking, mobile money integration, and a network of verified drivers, we're creating a platform that brings transparency, convenience, and trust to the trotro experience.

Our Mission

To digitize and modernize Ghana's trotro transport for safer, smarter, and fairer urban mobility.

Our Vision

A connected West Africa where public transport is efficient, inclusive, and technology-powered.

Our Impact

How We're Improving Trotro Transport

We tackle the biggest pain points in the trotro system with technology-driven solutions.

Reduced Wait Times

Real-time tracking lets passengers know exactly when the next trotro arrives — no more waiting for hours at the station.

Fair & Transparent Pricing

Digital fare calculation eliminates overcharging. Passengers and drivers agree on a fair price before the trip starts.

Enhanced Safety

GPS tracking, driver verification, and trip sharing features give passengers peace of mind on every journey.

Logistics Integration

Trotros become parcel carriers along their routes, creating a new income stream for drivers and affordable delivery for senders.

Driver Empowerment

Earnings dashboards, trip analytics, and financial tools help drivers manage and grow their income effectively.

Route Optimization

Data-driven route planning reduces congestion, lowers fuel costs, and minimizes the environmental impact of trotro transport.
