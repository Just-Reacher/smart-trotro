Everything You Need for
Smarter Transport

From real-time tracking to cashless payments, Smart-Trotro brings modern technology to Ghana's most-used public transport system.

Real-Time Trotro Tracking

Track trotros on your route in real time. Know exactly when the next bus arrives and plan your journey smarter.

Mobile Money Payments

Pay fares seamlessly with MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo Money. No more scrambling for change.

Parcel Delivery Along Routes

Send packages along existing trotro routes. Affordable, fast delivery leveraging the transport network already in place.

Driver Earnings Dashboard

Drivers get full visibility into their earnings, trip history, and performance metrics — all in one clean dashboard.

How It Works

Get Moving in 3 Simple Steps

Whether you're commuting or sending a parcel, Smart-Trotro makes it effortless.

1

Sign Up & Find Your Route

Create your free account and search for trotro routes near you. See real-time availability and estimated arrival times.

2

Book & Pay with Mobile Money

Reserve your seat or schedule a parcel pickup. Pay instantly with mobile money — no cash needed.

3

Ride or Track Your Parcel

Board your trotro or track your parcel in real time. Rate your experience and earn loyalty rewards.

About Smart-Trotro

Transforming How Ghana Moves

Smart-Trotro is on a mission to digitize and modernize Ghana's trotro transport system; the backbone of urban mobility for millions of Ghanaians.

We believe that technology can make public transport safer, more reliable, and more transparent for everyone- passengers, drivers, and businesses alike.

  • Safer, more transparent rides
  • Fair pricing with no overcharging
  • Income empowerment for drivers
  • Eco-friendly route optimization
